The Dynasty Scoreboard

A Different Approach to Weekly Scoring in Fantasy Football
example league more info

This scoreboard ranks all teams against each other for the given week, so no more default wins because you happened to match up against Rick, who always seems to forget that the NFL has games on Thursday nights now. With the weekly scoreboard, everyone has something to gain (or lose) up until the final whistle on Monday night; so yes, this may just redeem that Falcons @ Texans slumber party you were planning on skipping.
